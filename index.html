Welcome to Find Your Path!

A Visitor's Guide to U.S. National Parks

Attention all nature lovers! Find Your Path is an expert guide to locating all of the United State's National Parks. Simply search your location, or the state you would like to visit, and find all of the information necessary to plan your next hike!
Once your location or state has been determined, a map will load showing nearby U.S. National Parks. Below the map you will find a row of parks in your area or state. Simply click the button with the name of the park you want to visit, and you will see the parks's information below.
Enjoy your visit!
